About us

The Cake Shop was founded by a team of passionate and dedicated bakers who are committed in baking the most delicious cakes and pastries.
Using only the freshest ingredients we can find, you can be sure that you are served the best quality cake possible.

From the beginning we have chosen to work with local farmers, jam makers and beekeepers, because we believe local, quality ingredients are what set our food apart.
We are always striving to become even better bakers. We love what we do, and hope that comes through in every buttery, flaky bite.
